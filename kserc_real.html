Regulatory Rules Reference
Hardcoded KSERC rules applied to every analysis — these never change without a new regulation
Perennial Rejections (Always ₹0)
1. Section 3(1) Electricity Duty
Cannot be passed to consumers. Kerala Electricity Duty Act. No exceptions.
2. CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) Expenses
Appropriation of profit. Companies Act 2013, Section 135. Not a cost of distribution.
3. RPO Liability Provisions
Only actual REC payments allowed. Provisions/liabilities disallowed.
O&M Normative Cap
Cap = Prev.Approved × (1 + AICPI_rate)

Covers: Employee Cost + R&M + A&G
Regulation: KSERC Tariff Regs 2021, Reg. 12(3)

Exception: One-time documented expenses
may be approved above the cap
AT&C Loss & 2/3 Efficiency Rule
AT&C Loss = ((Input − Sold) / Input) × 100

If Actual Loss < Target:
Gain(MU) = (Target% − Actual%) × Input / 100
Savings = Gain(MU) × PP_Cost_per_unit
Licensee keeps: (2/3) × Savings
Core Revenue Gap Formula
Gap = Approved_ARR − Actual_Revenue

Positive → GAP (owed to licensee)
Negative → SURPLUS (excess collected)

Sanity bounds:
Max Surplus: ₹280.42L (Technopark 2020-21)
Max Gap: ₹64.72L (Infopark 2024-25)
